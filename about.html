I'm a person who loves the details. I love learning how things work, the logic behind a rule or best practice, and how to create things from scratch.

How this plays out in life is that when I am interested in something I take a deep dive.

My love of learning how things work and learning how to make things combined with my love of details are the reasons learning programming is a great fit for me! As I searched for a career that would utilize my natural talents and suit my mind set my research kept pointing towards programming.

As a detail-oriented person, before making a big life change and enrolling in a programming educational program, I began by exploring Epicodus' free online curriculum. And when this self-directed study confirmed that I enjoy programming, I enrolled in the Epicodus Boot Camp.
